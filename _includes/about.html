About

It all started with an idea that grew into something magical.

  • March 9th, 2018

    The Beginning

    BaggyNoodleArms had a dream of creating a safe place for Twitch streamers to work and support each other to reach the first milestone... to become a Twitch affiliate.

  • 2019

    A Dream to Reality

    As the dream became real the growth started. Our community became the home for many new members. We grew streamers. We had Twitch Con meetups. We even matched jerseys. The community grew to a family with the support needed to take our members to new heights.

  • March 9th, 2020

    Welcome to our Next Chapter

    Looking to the future we wanted to grow beyond Twitch. We are more inclusive to streamers, content creators, viewers, chatters, and everything in between. While shedding the "Twitch" name we still retain the community "crew" we've always strived to be. Creator Crew is the new chapter for us and we're proud to welcome you and all!

  • Future

    What's Next?

    Growth... we plan to support and grow all members of our "crew". We will strive to make this "crew" a family and support your ability to express your creativity, passion, and drive to become the artist, the creator, the streamer, or any other version of yourself that will benefit from a true family... a true "crew".
