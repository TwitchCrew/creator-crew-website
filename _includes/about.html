  • March 9th, 2018

    The Beginning

    BaggyNoodleArms had a dream of creating a safe place for Twitch Streamers to work and support eachother and their memebers to complete the first goal of every streamer, to get to affiliate.

  • 2019

    A Dream to Reality

    As time came and went, we grew. We became the home to many, watched many new streamers grow to be something bigger than when they first started out, we had meet ups at Twitch Con, matching jerseys, and one big happy family.

  • March 9th, 2020

    Welcome to our Next Chapter

    For our second anniversary, we wanted to grow beyond what we made. We wanted to be more inclusive to streamers, lurkers, and creators. Creator Crew is born as our new chapter. Even though we no longer are Twitch Crew, we will always be The Crew.

  • July 2014

    What's Next?

    We don't know! But we have always put you, our members and family, to the forefront of all of our plans and ideas. Making this a community a family. And as we grow as a family, you grow as an individual to express your creativity in ways you never thought possible, and giving us the opportunity to be there to support you.
